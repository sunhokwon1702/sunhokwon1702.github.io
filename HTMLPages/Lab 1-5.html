Almond Green Salad

Fresh and Crispy

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2tablespoons white wine vinegar
3tablespoons honey
1/2 teaspoon dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite-size pieces
1 can of mandarin oragnes, drained
1 bunch green onions, diced
3/4 cup silvered almoonds

Directions

1. In medium skillet over medium-heat, cook bacon until evenly browned. Drain cool, and crumble.

2. To make the dressing, throughly blend the vinegar, honey, dry mustrad, celery salt, paprika, and olive oil

3. Place lettuce, oranges, green onions, bacon, and almonds in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and serve.
